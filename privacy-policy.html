Privacy Policy

222 of Self Inquiry
Effective Date: April 1, 2026

🙏 Your privacy matters to us. This app is designed to support your self-inquiry practice, not to collect your data.

1. Overview

222 of Self Inquiry ("we", "our", "the app") is a meditation and self-inquiry practice tool based on the Advaita 222 Self-Inquiry™ teachings of Rajiv Kapur. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Practice Log — Records of your inquiry sessions (date, time, duration, outcome). This helps you track your practice.
  • Reminder Settings — Your preferred reminder times for daily practice.
  • App Preferences — Theme (light/dark) and voice guidance settings.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or cloud service.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo / React Native — The framework used to build the app. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and performance data. See Expo's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo Notifications — Used to schedule local reminders on your device. These are local notifications only — no data is sent to external servers.

5. Notifications

The app may request permission to send you local notifications for practice reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device. You can enable or disable them at any time through the app's Reminders settings or through your device's notification settings.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Links to External Websites

The app contains a link to rajivkapur.com. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can clear your practice log and settings by uninstalling the app.
